Design-Patterns-Project

This a design-pattern project for COS 214 of the University of Pretoria by the group TSP. We will model a Formula one team, specifically the logistics and racing, using at least 10 design patterns. 

The following patterns were used:

      1. Composite
      2. Decorator
      3. Prototype
      4. Factory
      5. Builder
      6. Memento
      7. Iterator
      8. State
      9. Observer
     10. Mediator
     11. Strategy

The project will be coded in C++ 20 and submitted on the 9th of Novemeber 2020.
